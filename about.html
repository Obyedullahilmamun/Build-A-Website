About


Bangladesh Army International University of Science and Technology (BAIUST), a university affiliated with the Bangladesh Army, started its journey on 14 February 2015.


4 year B.Sc. Undergraduate Engineering and Hon's Courses

B.Sc. Undergraduate Engineering Programs are:

  • Faculty of Electrical and Computer Engineering
    1. Electrical & Electronics Engineering
    2. Computer Science & Engineering.

  • Faculty of Civil Engineering
    1. Civil Engineering.

  • School of Business
    1. Business Administration.

  • Faculty of Science & Humanities
    1. Bachelor of Arts (Hon's) in English.
    2. Bachelor of Law (LLB).

  • ICT
  • Economics

Graduate Programs are:

  • MBA
  • M.A in ELT
